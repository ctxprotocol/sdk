Live US equities intelligence from Financial Modeling Prep: real-time quotes, financial statements, valuation, analyst consensus, technicals, market movers, and screening — plus 14 new alpha surfaces (earnings surprises & calendar, dividends, insider & congressional trades, institutional ownership, SEC filings, stock peers, financial growth, revenue segments, index constituents, DCF/enterprise valuation, quality scores, sector performance, and the macro calendar with the Treasury yield curve) in one MCP endpoint.

Features:
- Resolve tickers with symbol/name search, pull company profiles (sector, market cap, beta), and batch real-time quotes
- Income / balance sheet / cash flow statements and TTM or period-series financial ratios (ROE, margins, leverage)
- Earnings: actual vs estimated EPS/revenue with surprise delta, plus the forward earnings calendar
- Dividends history (ex-div, record, payment dates) and the dividends calendar
- Insider trading (Form 4 filer, transactionType, value) and congressional house/senate trades by politician
- Institutional ownership: top 13F holders, free float, outstanding shares (CIK auto-resolved to latest filing)
- SEC filings search by symbol plus latest 8-K material-event feed
- Stock peers (FMP comparables) and financial growth series (revenue, EBITDA, FCF, EPS YoY)
- Revenue segmentation by geography and product line
- S&P 500 / Nasdaq / Dow Jones constituent rosters with sector and sub-industry tags
- Valuation: DCF fair value vs price, enterprise value, EV/EBITDA, EV/sales, market cap, multi-period price change
- Quality scores: Altman Z, Piotroski F, Beneish M, working capital, plus the FMP rating grade
- Sector & industry performance (1D/5D/1M/YTD) with average P/E per sector and industry
- Macro economic calendar (FOMC, CPI, NFP) and the live Treasury yield curve in one call
- Analyst consensus: price-target, grade distribution, forward estimates with partial-error reporting
- Screen the universe by market cap, price, beta, volume, dividend, sector, industry, exchange, country
- Daily market movers (gainers, losers, most actives), EOD price history, technicals (RSI, SMA, EMA, ADX)
- build_company_financial_brief composites profile + quote + TTM ratios + analyst + news into one snapshot

Agent tips:
- Use search_symbols first when given a company name; byName=true for name search
- get_stock_quote takes a symbols array for multi-ticker comparisons
- For relative value, chain get_stock_peers -> get_valuation -> get_growth to rank a group cheapest-to-richest
- For catalyst-sentiment, combine get_earnings + get_insider_activity + get_congressional_trades on one ticker
- get_ownership auto-resolves CIK to the latest 13F filing period and returns ranked holders plus float
- get_macro_calendar returns the forward calendar AND the Treasury yield curve together
- get_sector_performance returns performance AND average P/E for all 11 sectors and their industries
- Analyst and some international endpoints may return partialErrors if the FMP tier lacks them; report those explicitly rather than guessing
- FMP plan rate limits apply; composite tools reduce redundant upstream calls
